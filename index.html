FAQ

Questions we get asked.

How do you access my email?

We use Google OAuth2... the same secure method used by Superhuman, Mixmax, and other email tools. We read email metadata and threads to understand your relationships. We never send emails on your behalf without your explicit action. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Is my data shared with anyone?

Never. Your contact data, email content, and relationship analysis are completely private to your account. We don’t sell data. We don’t train AI models on your emails.

How long does setup take?

About 5 minutes. Connect your Gmail and Calendar via OAuth, and we begin analyzing your production network immediately. Your first digest arrives the next business day. Full network tiering completes within 24–48 hours.

What if a suggested contact’s email bounces?

We verify every email before it reaches your digest. We check against LinkedIn profiles and company domains. If we can’t verify a high-confidence email, the contact won’t appear in your digest.

Can I customize what contacts are suggested?

Yes. You can set quarterly goals, target contact profiles (agency type, seniority, company size), outreach frequency preferences, and flag contacts as do-not-contact. The AI adapts to your preferences over time.

How is this different from a CRM?

CRMs track deals. We track relationships. A CRM tells you a project closed 18 months ago. We tell you that the creative director who brought you that project just moved to a bigger agency, has been posting about needing production partners, and here’s a draft email to congratulate them on the move. It’s a living system... constantly refreshing signals and surfacing new opportunities.

How is this different from outbound agencies like Surge?

They find new people. We resurface people who already know your work. Surge generates cold meetings with creative directors who’ve never seen your reel. We generate warm conversations with creative directors who’ve already sat in your edit suite. They’re actually complementary... Surge for new, us for existing.

Do you support multiple team members?

Yes. Each user gets their own digest calibrated to their voice and goals. Great for production companies where the founder and an EP or head of sales each manage their own relationships.
