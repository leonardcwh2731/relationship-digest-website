  • Your agency is in the $2M–$10M ARR range and you’re looking to scale.
  • You’ve been running your video production agency long enough to have thousands of dormant relationships with real revenue potential.
  • Your inbox and calendar are your primary communication layer… not a CRM, not social DMs, not scattered across platforms.
  • You want more… not because you’re struggling, but because you know your network is underutilized.
  • You believe the best client relationships are built through genuine care, not transaction-first outreach.

This is not for you if…

  • You’re looking for a self-service tool to configure and leave running without engagement.

  • You’d compare this to a LinkedIn automation tool or an email outreach agency… it isn’t either.
  • You have a scarcity mindset about your network and your market.

FAQ

Questions we get asked.

How do you access my email and is my data private?

We connect via Google OAuth with read-only access. We extract contact signals and destroy everything else immediately. Storing your emails doesn’t make our product better, in fact, it makes it slow and expensive. Also, we can never send, delete, or modify anything in your inbox. We don’t have a dashboard where anyone reads your threads, and we don’t sell or share your data. You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.

How long does setup take?

Everything happens on one 45-minute discovery call. We walk you through the product and capture your goals, and you would pay and connect your Gmail and Calendar and receive your first digest within 30 minutes.

What if a suggested contact’s email bounces?

Every contact goes through email enrichment and verification before it appears in your digest. We check against LinkedIn profiles and company domains. If we can’t verify a high-confidence email, the contact won’t appear. If one slips through, let us know and we’ll troubleshoot it.

Can I customize what contacts are suggested?

Yes. During your discovery call, we talk through your quarterly goals, ideal company, and ideal client profile. That context goes directly into the system before your first digest. Over time, the system also learns from your outreach... which angles get replies, which contact types convert... and adjusts accordingly.

What happens on the monthly call?

Every month we review your snapshot together... digests sent, contacts reached, replies, meetings booked, and which signals are converting. We use that session to tune your account... adjusting signal weights, refining your ICP context, updating draft patterns. It’s not a status update. It’s a calibration that makes the next month sharper than the last.

Is this a self-service tool?

No. Relationship Digest is a managed service. We handle the onboarding, the network scan, the signal monitoring, and the monthly calibration. You receive a weekday digest and act on it. No dashboard. No logins. No manual data entry. The system runs. You reach out.

How is this different from a CRM?

CRMs track deals. We track relationships. A CRM tells you a project closed 18 months ago. We tell you that the creative director who brought you that project just moved to a bigger agency, has been posting about needing production partners, and here’s a draft email to congratulate them on the move. It’s a living system... constantly refreshing signals and surfacing new opportunities.

How is this different from outbound agencies like Surge?

They find new people. We resurface people who already know your work. Surge generates cold meetings with creative directors who’ve never seen your reel. We generate warm conversations with creative directors who’ve already sat in your edit suite. They’re actually complementary... Surge for new, us for existing.
